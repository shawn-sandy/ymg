We’re so glad you’re here! **You Me and the Gospel** is a place where life and faith intersect, offering a space to explore, reflect, and grow together. Each week, we host meaningful conversations about the Gospel, life’s challenges, and everything in between.

What makes us unique? Our platform is powered by innovation and faith. Our weekly podcast is generated using AI from live session audio, complete with AI-crafted summaries, show notes, and discussions of Bible passages. It’s designed to make diving into the Word of God both engaging and accessible.

Whether you’re tuning in to the podcast, exploring our resources, or simply seeking connection, we’re excited to have you on this journey. Let’s navigate life together through the lens of the Gospel.

Welcome to the community!
